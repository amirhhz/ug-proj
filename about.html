Where: University of Cambridge, UK

When: Academic Year 2010-11

What: A blog covering mainly the progress of my final year project and dissertation on recommender systems in social networks, and the inevitable off-topic meanderings.

The Long Version

I study Computer Science at the University of Cambridge. Currently (2010-11) I am in my final year of the undergraduate course and I intend to use this blog to document my progress in carrying out my dissertation project, focusing on recommendation engines, hopefully in a social networking context. I'll probably be changing the design and structure of the blog as I write more, so I welcome friendly suggestions for a more meaning tagline, Wordpress themes and plugins to try out, or whatever.
